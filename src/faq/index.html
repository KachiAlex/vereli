FAQ

Common questions

Is Vereli a CRM?

No. Vereli focuses on managing client work and collaboration, not sales pipelines.

Is Vereli a project management tool?

Partly, but Vereli is designed specifically for service businesses managing ongoing client work.

Who is Vereli for?

Agencies, consultants, communications firms, studios, advisory teams, media companies, and other service businesses.

Can I customize workflows?

Yes. You can create your own work stages and service structures.

Can clients collaborate inside Vereli?

Yes. Each client workspace includes a built-in client portal. Clients receive a portal link and can log in to review work, leave feedback, and approve deliverables — without needing a Vereli team account.
