Common questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or change your plan anytime as your business grows.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every plan comes with a 14-day free trial.

Do clients get their own access?

Yes. Every client workspace includes a client portal. You share a portal link with your client — they can log in to review files, leave feedback, and approve deliverables without needing a Vereli team seat.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Vereli runs directly in your browser.

What happens after the free trial?

You can choose a paid plan to continue using Vereli. Your data stays intact.

Which payment providers are supported?

Vereli supports Paystack, Flutterwave, and Stripe for collecting payments from clients through their portal.
